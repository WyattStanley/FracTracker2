Washington State


    • Crude Oil Spill Risk


    This project was created to explore the effects of crude oil transportation across Washington State.

    This project considers the effects of three modes of crude oil transportation and spill risk on Washington residents and their environment.



    Pipelines

    northwest pipeline project, pipeline environmental impact

    Trains

    routes, spills, environmental impact, population impact

    Vessels

    spill risk mitigation, coastal protection, habitat protection
